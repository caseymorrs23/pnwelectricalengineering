Vale Apartments — Shared-Circuit EV Charging Upgrade

Location: Bellevue, Washington

Client: Resound Energy
Location: Bellevue, Washington
Project Type: EV Charging Infrastructure
PE Stamp: WA PE 55998 • OR PE 108239PE

Project Overview

Vale Apartments required a scalable EV charging solution in an underground garage with structural constraints. The goal was to design a shared-circuit layout that maximized stall coverage while minimizing electrical upgrades and avoiding disruptive core drilling.

Engineering Approach

PNW Electrical Engineering developed a shared-circuit Wallbox design tied into the existing electrical room. Conduit routing and homerun lengths were carefully planned to maintain acceptable voltage drop while preserving future expansion options.

Key Technical Highlights

  • Shared-circuit architecture to support multiple Wallbox chargers efficiently.
  • Routing strategy built around existing building structure and limitations.
  • Panel capacity checks and voltage-drop calculations for long homeruns.
  • Template-based design approach to support future phases at the same site.

Deliverables

  • PE-stamped engineering submittal package.
  • One-line diagram.
  • Voltage-drop and load calculations.
  • Panel schedules.
  • Charger layout notes and routing recommendations.
